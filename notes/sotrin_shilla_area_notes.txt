General Creature notes for all areas:
   ah = average hit (assumes best armor and shield)
   at = # of attacks by creature (helps understand why 
        'consider' may say you can survive, only factors first attack)
   K  = thousand (used in point estimates)
   lp = life points of creature (aka hit points on 
        Nightblade's site)
   mh = max hit 
       (please note:  this only represents the amount shown safe
                      using consider.  Under certain random conditions 
                      the amount could be higher, but infrequent.
   xp = eXperience points
